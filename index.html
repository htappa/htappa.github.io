About Me

I am Henry Tappa, a seasoned Data Scientist with over 8 years of experience in the technology and renewable energy sectors. My journey in data science began with a strong foundation in statistical analysis and machine learning, which I have honed through hands-on experience in leading data-driven projects that drive business growth and innovation.

I hold a Master of Science in Data Science from George Washington University, which has equipped me with the technical and analytical skills necessary to tackle complex problems. Throughout my career, I have developed a deep proficiency in Python, SQL, and R, leveraging these tools to build robust machine learning models, design and implement advanced database systems, and create insightful data visualizations.

My professional experience spans various roles where I have successfully led the development of analytical frameworks, managed end-to-end machine learning projects, and collaborated with cross-functional teams to optimize processes and deliver actionable insights. I have a passion for clean energy and have worked extensively in this field, contributing to projects that support sustainable energy solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a strong advocate for continuous learning and mentorship, fostering growth and professional development within the teams I work with. My commitment to excellence and innovation drives me to continuously seek out new challenges and opportunities to make a meaningful impact through data science.
